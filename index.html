Animotions

This was a group project I worked on called Animotions. We created an augmented reality application meant to help reinforce the idea of emotion for children with autism. At a young age it is important to grow the understanding of emotion which is used in your day to day life. By creating this tool for teachers and therapists it will help establish a solid foundation using examples and practice lessons to help teach and foster some of the most important emotions.
